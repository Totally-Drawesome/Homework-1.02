BACKGROUND

Shrek is massive green ogre who is grumpy and crude on the outside but kind-hearted on the inside. Due to the public's perception of orges, Shrek preffered to isolate himself from society, but after befriending Princess Fiona and a talking Donkey, learned the importance of opening one's self to others. Shrek's many achomplishments include rescuing the princess from a dragon's keep, overthrowing corrupt politician Lord Farquaad, and liberating the kingdom of Far Far Away on numerous occasions. He quickly became a hero amoung the fairy-tale creatures, and even managed to earn the respect of the king and queen. Despite marrying into the royal family, Shrek ultimately chose to live a peaceful life in the swamp with his wife and three children.
